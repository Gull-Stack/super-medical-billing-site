Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Super Medical Billing.

Super Medical Billing is a modern, HIPAA-compliant medical billing platform built specifically for behavioral health practices. It handles the entire revenue cycle — claims submission (EDI 837P), real-time eligibility verification (270/271), electronic remittance advice (835), accounts receivable management, denial tracking & appeals, and provider credentialing — all in one unified system.

Yes, completely. HIPAA compliance is built into every layer of the platform. We use AES-256-GCM encryption for all PHI at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit, role-based access controls with JWT authentication, and maintain complete audit logs of every data access and modification. Our infrastructure is hosted on SOC 2 compliant servers. We provide a standard Business Associate Agreement (BAA) to all clients. See our Compliance page for full details.

We support all major commercial payers including Aetna, BlueCross BlueShield, UnitedHealthcare, Cigna, Optum, Humana, Medicare, Medicaid, and more. Claims are submitted through integrated clearinghouse connections (Claim.MD) for maximum payer coverage. If a payer accepts electronic claims, we can submit to them.

Super Medical Billing integrates directly with Claim.MD for claims submission and with Availity for real-time eligibility verification. Claims are generated in standard EDI 837P format and submitted electronically. Remittance advice (835/ERA) is imported automatically and matched to claims. You don't need a separate clearinghouse account — it's all built in.

All standard CPT codes are supported. For behavioral health, we come pre-loaded with common psychotherapy codes including 90834 (45-min individual), 90837 (60-min individual), 90847 (family therapy), 90832 (30-min individual), 90846 (family without patient), 90853 (group therapy), 90791 (diagnostic evaluation), and 96132 (neuropsych testing). You can add any additional CPT or HCPCS codes as needed.

We perform daily encrypted backups with point-in-time recovery capability. Backups are stored in geographically separate locations from the primary database. In the event of data loss, we can restore to any point within the retention window. All backups are encrypted with the same AES-256-GCM standard as the live database.
