GullStack Trust was founded with a simple mission: build technology that saves small businesses money. When we started working with behavioral health practices, we noticed something: the billing side was a disaster. Practices were juggling multiple disconnected systems, losing money on denied claims, and spending hours on manual processes that should have been automated years ago.
